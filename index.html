Why Lumina

A Dental Experience
You'll Actually Look Forward To

We know most people don't love going to the dentist. We've built our entire practice around changing that — and it shows in our 4.9-star rating from over 380 patients.

🏅

AHPRA Registered & ADA Member Practice

All our dentists are registered with the Australian Health Practitioner Regulation Agency (AHPRA). Lumina is a proud member of the Australian Dental Association — meaning we adhere to the highest standards of clinical practice and professional ethics.

😌

Anxiety-Free, Gentle Dentistry

Dental anxiety affects over 1 in 6 Australians. Every aspect of our practice — from our calm, spa-like environment to our communication style and sedation options — is designed to make nervous patients feel completely safe. We never rush, we always explain, and we always listen.

🔬

Advanced Technology — Better Outcomes

We invest in technology that improves your treatment: digital X-rays (90% less radiation than traditional), intraoral cameras for real-time diagnosis, CBCT 3D scanning for implant planning, and same-day CAD/CAM ceramic crowns using our CEREC machine — no temporary crowns, no second visit.

💰

No Hidden Costs — Transparent Treatment Plans

Before any treatment begins, you receive a written treatment plan with a full itemised cost breakdown. We process HICAPS on the spot and offer flexible payment plans. The price we quote is the price you pay — no surprise fees after treatment.

👨‍👩‍👧

The Whole Family Under One Roof

From toddlers to grandparents, we treat every member of your family. Our paediatric dentist Dr Patel is specially trained to make children's visits positive and fear-free — building healthy dental habits from the first visit. Family-block appointments available on Saturdays.

🚨

Same-Day Emergency Appointments

Dental emergencies don't wait for a convenient appointment slot. We hold same-day spots every weekday for pain, trauma, broken teeth and lost restorations. Call us before 10am and we will see you the same day. After-hours emergency triage available by phone 7 days.

✨

Award-Winning Cosmetic Results

Dr Chen is a recognised cosmetic dentistry specialist with advanced training at the prestigious AACD in the US. Our cosmetic outcomes are held to the standard of leading international clinics — with results that are indistinguishable from your natural teeth and built to last decades.

🌿

Sustainable, Eco-Conscious Practice

Lumina is a certified eco-friendly dental practice — we use digital records, biodegradable disposables, a filtered water recycling system, and energy-efficient LED operatory lighting throughout. We believe being kind to patients and kind to the planet aren't mutually exclusive.
